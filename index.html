Built on Partnership

At NES, our company is defined by more than just technical specifications—it is defined by the people we care for. We are three partners who spent years mastering our respective skillsets, working tirelessly in our past lives until, by some miracle, we found each other. We built this company on a foundation of mutual respect, family values, and a shared obsession with high-quality work.

We know what it takes to succeed in this industry, and we bring that hard-earned dedication to every project. Whether it’s waterproofing, siding, or complex glazing, we approach our work with the same care and integrity we use to support our own families. To us, a building envelope isn't just a structure; it’s our reputation and our legacy.

  • Mastered Skillsets: Three partners, decades of experience, and a unified vision for quality.
  • Relentless Commitment: We work as hard for your project as we did to build our own dreams.
  • Family & Values: We lead with respect, care for our team, and prioritize integrity above all else.

Technical precision meets genuine collaboration. We don’t just build high-performance envelopes; we build lasting relationships based on trust, grit, and the belief that how we work matters just as much as what we build.

Featured Services

Precon

Comprehensive pre-construction services leveraging advanced estimation, value engineering, and strategic planning to optimize budgets, mitigate risks, and streamline project schedules before breaking ground.

Commercial Siding & Panels

Expert installation of high-performance architectural and decorative panel systems combined with comprehensive air, vapor, and water barrier systems designed to endure the demanding climate while elevating design intent.

Glazing

High-performance architectural glazing and curtain wall systems engineered for maximum thermal efficiency and structural integrity. We deliver sleek, modern glass solutions that maximize natural light and elevate design intent while providing robust protection against the elements.
